Hi, I’m Leonie, a PhD student in math at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in the Sciences in Leipzig. I’m a member of the Nonlinear Algebra group, my advisor is Simon Telen. I am interested in (Commutative) Algebra and Algebraic Geometry with a view towards computation and applications.

I studied both mathematics and computer science at Leibniz University Hannover from 2016 to 2022. I wrote my master’s theses on The Waring problem for polynomials under Matthias Schütt and on Gröbner Bases and Their Complexity under Heribert Vollmer.
